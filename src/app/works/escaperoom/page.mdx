<hr />
For a school assignment, I developed an escape room using Blender and Unity. The escape room takes place on a mysterious, dark island. The player must collect fuel scattered across the island to power a generator. Once the generator is working, the tower lights are ignited, enabling a safe escape from the island. The 3D models were primarily created in Blender, while the game mechanics and code were implemented in Unity.
